How to Suggest a Resource:

To ensure quality and trackability, we manage new resource suggestions directly through our GitHub repository's issue tracker.

  1. Click the button below to go to our GitHub Issues page.
  2. Click "New Issue" and select the "Suggest a New Resource" template.
  3. Fill in the details (Course Name, Provider, Link, Key Topics, Credential Type, etc.).
  4. Submit the issue! Our team will review it and add it to the explorer.
